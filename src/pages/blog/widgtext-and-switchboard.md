WidgText and Switchboard serve different use cases, but the product standard is the same in both places: make the primary action obvious, reduce friction around repeat use, and keep support channels easy to find.

WidgText focuses on personal expression and flexible widget styling. Switchboard focuses on speed, organization, and making frequent audio-routing changes less tedious.

Treating the site as a blog first makes it easier to explain that work over time. Evergreen product pages stay stable, while posts can handle updates, release notes, and in-progress reasoning.
