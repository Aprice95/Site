WidgText started with a small frustration: widgets on iOS look great, but they rarely feel *yours*. I wanted the words that matter to me — a reminder, a favorite quote, a running joke — sitting on my Home Screen, styled the way I'd style them. So I built it, and today it's live on the App Store. 🎉

![WidgText widgets on an iPhone Home Screen](/images/apps/widgtext-shot-1.jpg)

## What it does

You type anything, and WidgText turns it into a widget. From there it's all about making it feel personal:

- A **live-preview editor** — type it, style it, see it instantly
- **Fonts** with bold and italic options, **multi-color gradients**, and border styles from clean solid to neon
- **Quote Mode**, which rotates through your own list of quotes or reminders automatically
- **Independent widget slots**, each with its own text, style, and schedule
- Widgets for the **Home Screen and Lock Screen**, so your words are there at a glance

## Free to start, cheap to unlock

The free tier gives you a full widget slot with core styling. WidgText Pro — every font, every gradient and border style, every widget slot, unlimited quotes, and no ads — is a **one-time $0.99 purchase**. No subscription.

One more thing that matters to me: **WidgText collects no data.** Your words stay on your device, period.
