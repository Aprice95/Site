Simple design is not about removing personality. It is about making the important parts of a product easier to understand and easier to trust.

When navigation is clear and language is direct, people spend less time guessing and more time getting value from the experience itself.

That same thinking shapes product pages, onboarding, support, and every place where a user decides whether a product feels reliable.
